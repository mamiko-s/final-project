About

Hello, I am Mamiko from Japan! In the past 2 years, I worked as a web content writer/creator and created FAQ content about IoT products as well as a timeline article for apps user. During that job I was always able to use templates that were already prepared so I didn't have to create from zero. Then I always wondered how this web content or application was built or the meaning of coding but I only knew basic understanding of HTML/CSS. So I started into more developer side and this is the reason I started studying programming.

I am currently taking Web development courses at Tamwood Careers in Vancouver. In this course, I will be able to design and code to create dynamic websites using HTML/CSS and JavaScript as well as a content management system. I am expected to complete my courses by end of September and after my courses, I am planning to become a Front-end developer. I am studying hard at school and also taking online courses such as Udemy and Linkedin learning to achieve my goal. As I learn more about coding I realized how interesting it is.
